Our Goal

At REarth, our goal is to inspire and mobilize individuals, communities, and organizations worldwide to take immediate and impactful action on climate change, fostering a collective movement towards a sustainable, resilient, and equitable planet for future generations. Through education, awareness, innovative solutions, and collaborative partnerships, we aim to contribute significantly to achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and resolving global environmental challenges.

Responsible Earthlings for a Sustainable Planet

Dedicated to fostering climate education, awareness, and action, we empower individuals, especially youth, with knowledge and tools to promote sustainability and eco-conscious actions. Through education, collaboration, and innovation, we bridge the gap between academic insights and real-world climate solutions. Our programs, such as the SDG Star School, Women in Climate Leadership, and other educational workshops, engage communities to take collective action towards a sustainable future.

What is the Climate Clock?
The Climate Clock deadline shows how long we have left until the Earth's carbon budget runs out. It visually represents the time remaining to stay within our carbon budget and avoid surpassing critical temperature thresholds. The clock serves as a stark reminder of the urgency to take immediate and substantial action to reduce CO₂ emissions.
