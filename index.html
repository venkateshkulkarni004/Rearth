Our Goal

At REarth, our goal is to inspire and mobilize individuals, communities, and organizations worldwide to take immediate and impactful action on climate change, fostering a collective movement towards a sustainable, resilient, and equitable planet for future generations. Through education, awareness, innovative solutions, and collaborative partnerships, we aim to contribute significantly to achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and resolving global environmental challenges.

DID YOU KNOW?

2024: The Hottest Year

Global temperatures in 2024 exceeded the critical 1.5°C threshold, marking it as the hottest year ever recorded.

Planetary Boundaries

Scientists warn that we have already breached 6 out of 9 planetary boundaries, pushing Earth's systems beyond safe limits and threatening global stability.

Triple Planetary Crisis

Climate change, Biodiversity collapse, and Pollution are the main three interconnected planetary crises that impact ecosystems, human health, and economies.

Your Impact Matters

Research indicates that when just 25% of a population adopts a new behavior, it can trigger a societal shift, leading the majority to follow suit.
