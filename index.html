Our Goal

At REarth, our goal is to inspire and mobilize individuals, communities, and organizations worldwide to take immediate and impactful action on climate change, fostering a collective movement towards a sustainable, resilient, and equitable planet for future generations. Through education, awareness, innovative solutions, and collaborative partnerships, we aim to contribute significantly to achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and resolving global environmental challenges.

Community Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) for Foreign Residents

We are excited to share that recently we had our first preliminary meeting of the Indian community members to discuss developing a (Local) Community Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) Plan for non-Japanese speakers of our area in Yokohama, Japan. The need for such a program was unanimously recognized, and many valuable ideas were exchanged.

Living in an earthquake-prone country, foreign residents often struggle with access to critical disaster information, preparedness measures, and communication platforms. Through this initiative, we aim to bridge this gap and create a structured DRR plan tailored to the needs of non-Japanese speakers.

At REarth, we believe that community-led action is key to building resilience in a changing world. Through Women in Climate Leadership (#WICL), we are focusing on #DRR activities to empower and prepare ourselves and communities together. Our vision is to collaborate with key stakeholders—local government, embassy, fire departments, universities, and training institutions—to make this initiative a reality.

How You Can Support This Initiative:
Expert Guidance If you know any individuals, organizations, or resources that can help or guide us, please connect us.
