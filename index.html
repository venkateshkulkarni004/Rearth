About Climate Clock

A real-time reminder of the urgency to act against climate change.

What is the Carbon Budget?

The carbon budget works like a financial budget but for emissions. It represents the maximum CO₂ we can release while keeping global warming within safe limits.

Why is it Important?

Exceeding the carbon budget means triggering extreme weather events, rising sea levels, and ecosystem destruction. Staying within limits is crucial for the planet's future.

Where Do We Stand?

Annual global CO₂ emissions: 42.2 Gt per year
Remaining carbon budget: 400 Gt before we exceed the 1.5°C threshold

Source: IPCC AR6 Report

How Can We Make a Difference?

  • Reduce carbon footprint
  • Support renewable energy
  • Adopt plant-based diets
  • Avoid overconsumption
  • Advocate for climate policies
