SDG STAR SCHOOL

Transforming Education for a Sustainable Future

Education must evolve to meet the challenges of today’s world. The SDG Star School Program (3SP) is a pioneering initiative that embeds sustainability, climate science, and global responsibility into school curriculums, creating a new generation of change-makers and problem-solvers.

What makes SDG Star School unique?

Unlike other programs that merely introduce SDGs into the curriculum, 3SP drives systemic and academic change through a whole-school approach—empowering students, training educators, and engaging communities for lasting impact.



What Does 3SP Do?

The SDG Star School Program works across multiple levels to ensure deep integration of sustainability in education:

  • Whole-School Approach – Embedding sustainability into every aspect of learning, operations, and governance.
  • Curriculum Integration – Linking SDG principles with STEM, humanities, and social sciences to make education more relevant.
  • Teacher Training & Capacity Building – Equipping educators with innovative pedagogical tools and strategies to teach SDGs effectively.
  • Research & Innovation Labs – Creating school-based labs for climate research, eco-tech projects, and sustainability experiments.
  • Eco-Clubs & Student Action Groups – Encouraging students to lead real-world projects in their schools and communities.
  • Partnerships & Collaborations – Connecting schools with global sustainability networks, NGOs, and academic institutions.
  • Impact Measurement & Recognition – Helping schools track progress, apply for awards, grants, and global certifications.

Leadership

Why Partner with REarth for the SDG Star School Program?

Implementing SDG education effectively requires expertise, structured systems, and continuous engagement—which is where REarth comes in. Schools may have the intent, but without the right framework, these initiatives often remain fragmented or unsustainable.

Expertise & Guidance

We bring specialized knowledge in SDG integration, climate education, and ESD (Education for Sustainable Development), ensuring that your school implements a globally recognized model rather than ad-hoc initiatives.

Structured System

We provide our own IDEAS (Identify, design, Execute, Assess and Sustain) Model to embed sustainability into curriculum, operations, and student engagement, ensuring a holistic transformation.

Easing Workload

We recognize that teachers already have heavy responsibilities. REarth supports implementation, provides training, and creates student-led initiatives, so sustainability becomes a natural part of school life without burdening educators.

Engaging Students

Sustainability is not just theory—our program empowers students through Eco Clubs, Research Labs, and experiential learning, keeping them actively involved and future-ready.

Brand Positioning

Schools excelling in sustainability education stand out in a competitive market. Being an SDG Star School enhances reputation, attracts eco-conscious parents, and aligns with global education trends.

Global Recognition

Through our network, we connect schools to international awards, funding opportunities, and global education initiatives, providing visibility and credibility beyond local outreach.

By working with REarth, schools don’t just “implement SDGs”—they become recognized leaders in sustainability education, driving real impact while benefiting from expert guidance and strategic support.
