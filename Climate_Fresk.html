The Climate Canvas

Climate Fresk Workshop by REarth


The Challenge

Climate science can be overwhelming, but breaking it down into an interactive and visual experience makes it accessible to all. Raising awareness and fostering a collective understanding of climate change is key to driving meaningful action.

The Approach

The Climate Fresk workshop employs a collaborative, game-based method using 42 cards derived from IPCC reports. Participants work together to link causes and effects, constructing a visual representation—or "fresk"—of climate change mechanisms.

The Outcome

Attendees gain a solid foundation in climate science, empowering them to take meaningful action within their personal and professional spheres. The workshop also fosters critical thinking and collaborative problem-solving skills.
