The Climate Canvas: Climate Fresk

Climate science can be overwhelming, but breaking it down into an interactive and visual experience makes it accessible to all. Raising awareness and fostering a collective understanding of climate change is key to driving meaningful action.

The Challenge

Climate science can be overwhelming, but breaking it down into an interactive and visual experience makes it accessible to all. Raising awareness and fostering a collective understanding of climate change is key to driving meaningful action.

The Approach

The Outcome

Logistics

  • Duration: 3 hours.
  • Format: Interactive and collaborative; available both in-person and online.
  • Participants: Suitable for businesses, NGOs, educational institutions, community groups, students, corporate teams, and sustainability professionals.
  • Facilitator: The workshop is led by our trained, enthusiastic, and adaptable facilitator, ensuring an engaging and insightful learning experience.
