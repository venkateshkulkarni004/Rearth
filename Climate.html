Climate Fresk Workshop

Climate Fresk is a collaborative workshop that uses collective intelligence to understand climate change. Through interactive learning and creative problem-solving, we empower individuals and organizations to take meaningful climate action.

What is Climate Fresk?

A 3-hour serious game where participants work together to create connections between climate change causes and impacts. Based on IPCC reports, this science-based approach helps people grasp climate complexity while fostering collaboration and solution-building.

Why Participate?

Join over 1 million participants worldwide who have gained climate literacy through Fresk workshops. Learn key leverage points for action, understand systemic connections, and become part of a global movement driving climate solutions.

Workshop Features

Science-Based

Rooted in IPCC climate reports and peer-reviewed research

Collaborative
