Important Emergency Numbers in Japan

Stay informed with emergency numbers and resources in Japan.

ServiceNumberDetails
Police110For reporting crimes, accidents, or if you need police assistance. Required for legal/insurance documentation after accidents.
Fire119For reporting fires. Say (fire) if possible. English support via interpreter.
Ambulance119For medical emergencies. Say (ambulance) if possible. English support via interpreter.
Non-urgent Medical#7119For medical advice when unsure if an ambulance is needed. English support may be available.
Coast Guard118For maritime emergencies or trouble at sea within Japanese waters.
Disaster Safety Confirmation171Voice message board during disasters (earthquakes, etc.). Record or check safety messages for family/friends in Japan.
Report Child Abuse189To report child abuse or seek help if you are struggling with childcare stress.
