Actionable Climate Policies: En-ROADS Climate Workshop

Addressing climate change requires a comprehensive understanding of the complex interplay between energy, economy, and environmental policies. The En-ROADS Climate Workshop utilizes a dynamic, interactive climate model to explore how policies, technologies, and behavioral shifts can work together to build a sustainable future.

The Challenge

Understanding climate solutions can feel complex, but with the right tools, we can make informed decisions that drive impactful change. Many individuals and organizations seek clarity on how policies, technologies, and behavioral shifts can work together to build a sustainable future.

The Approach

Utilizing the En-ROADS simulatorâ€”a dynamic, interactive climate model developed by Climate Interactive and MIT Sloanâ€”participants can test various policy and solution scenarios in real-time. This hands-on approach fosters a deeper grasp of how different actions impact global temperature and climate outcomes.

The Outcome

Participants emerge with a nuanced understanding of the levers influencing climate change and are better equipped to develop and implement strategies that contribute to limiting global warming to well below 2Â°C, in line with international agreements.

Logistics

  • Duration: Approximately 2 hours.
  • Format: Interactive workshop suitable for both in-person and online settings.
  • Participants: Ideal for individuals, students, corporate teams, policymakers, educators, and sustainability professionals.
  • Facilitator: The workshop is led by our trained, enthusiastic, and adaptable facilitator, ensuring an engaging and insightful learning experience.
