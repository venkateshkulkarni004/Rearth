Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rearth?

Rearth is a non-profit organization based in Yokohama and Tokyo, focusing on climate change education and sustainability initiatives.

What programs does Rearth offer?

Rearth offers a variety of programs including SDG Star School, After School Program, Climate Warrior Club, ENROADS workshops, Climate Fresk, Biodiversity workshops, Women in Climate Leadership, and Ready Japan - DRRPF.

How can I get involved?

You can get involved by participating in our programs, volunteering, or supporting our initiatives. Contact us for more information.
