Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rearth?

Rearth is a social enterprise (currently in the process of registration) based in Yokohama and Tokyo, focusing on climate change education and sustainability initiatives.

What programs does Rearth offer?

REarth offers the SDG Star School Program, teacher training, game-based climate workshops, after-school clubs, and sustainability initiatives for schools and communities.

How can I get involved?

You can get involved by participating in our programs, volunteering, or supporting our initiatives. Contact us for more information.
