Women in Climate Leadership

At WICL, we believe that leadership is not just about titles—it's about influence, courage, and resilience. Through insightful discussions, skill-building sessions, and a strong support network, we are shaping a world where women's voices in climate and sustainability matter.

Why Women in Climate Leadership?

Climate change is no longer a distant threat—it is our reality. With rising global temperatures, frequent disasters, and increasing social vulnerabilities, the need for adaptation and resilience has never been greater. And who understands resilience better than women? Women have always been at the heart of adapting, sustaining, and rebuilding societies. At WICL—Women in Climate Leadership—we recognize and amplify this strength, creating a space where women can step into leadership roles with confidence. We believe that women are not just participants in climate solutions; they are leaders, decision-makers, and catalysts for change.

Why Climate Leadership Over Climate Action?

Action is the heart of any initiative, but leadership ensures lasting impact. At WICL, our goal is to unlock the immense potential of women, empowering them to make informed decisions—whether for their families, organizations, or communities—and lead with confidence. Women bring vision, influence, and inclusive decision-making to climate solutions, ensuring that sustainability is woven into every aspect.


Our Objectives

Learning and Dialogue

Creating a platform for learning and dialogue on climate leadership and disaster resilience.

Women's Empowerment

Strengthening women's roles in climate and sustainability.

Leadership and Innovation

Encouraging leadership and innovation in climate adaptation and sustainability.

Global Network

Building a global network of empowered women working towards a just and resilient future.
