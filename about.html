REarth

Responsible Earthlings for a Sustainable Planet is dedicated to fostering climate awareness, action, and driving positive change. We empower individuals, especially youth, with knowledge and tools to promote sustainability and eco-conscious actions through education, collaboration, and innovation.

Mission

To empower children and communities with the knowledge, tools, resources, and actionable solutions for climate awareness and sustainability. Through education, research, and collaboration, we bridge the gap between academic insights and real-world action, enabling a collective effort towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), building adaptation and resilience, and combating the climate crisis.

Vision

A thriving and sustainable planet where diversity flourishes, planetary crises are resolved, and individuals and communities unite to combat climate change, respect nature, and build a resilient future.

Values

  • Scientific Integrity: We ensure that all information shared is accurate, scientific, and factual.
  • Planetary Responsibility: We take collective ownership for creating a sustainable planet by promoting eco-conscious actions.
  • Resource Efficiency: Our programs are designed to utilize minimal resources while maximizing impact.
  • Energy and Resource Consciousness: We prioritize mindful use of energy and resources.
  • Power of Education: We believe that education drives meaningful change and empowers individuals to act.

Our Goal

To inspire and mobilize individuals, communities, and organizations worldwide to take immediate and impactful action on climate change, respect nature, and build resilience for a sustainable future.

Overview

The Biodiversity Workshop explores the critical relationship between biodiversity and climate change. By examining the impact of human activities on ecosystems, participants learn how protecting biodiversity can help mitigate climate change, ensure ecosystem services, and safeguard human wellbeing. This workshop provides a comprehensive understanding of biodiversity and introduces strategies businesses can adopt to enhance conservation efforts.
