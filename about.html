About Us

REarth

Responsible Earthlings for a Sustainable Planet is dedicated to fostering climate education, awareness, action, and driving positive change. We empower individuals, especially youth, with knowledge and tools to promote sustainability and eco-conscious actions. Through education, collaboration, and innovation, we bridge the gap between academic insights and real-world climate solutions. Our programs, such as the SDG Star School, Women in Climate Leadership and other educational workshops, engage communities to take collective action towards a sustainable future.

Message from Founder

We are living in one of the most challenging yet opportunity-filled times in human history. This decisive decade will determine the future of our planet, and every action—big or small—matters. Whether you are a large organization or a small business, the choices you make today will shape a sustainable tomorrow.

At REarth, we equip you with the knowledge and tools to make informed decisions that lead to meaningful climate action. Our workshops go beyond theory—they are interactive, science-backed, and solution-driven, designed to help organizations tackle environmental challenges effectively.

We believe that small, consistent efforts lead to lasting impact. By engaging communities, businesses, and individuals, we strive to build a network of environmentally conscious leaders dedicated to protecting our planet.

Join us in creating a responsible, climate-conscious future! Together, we can make a difference.

– Dr. Bageshree K., Founder, REarth

Vision

A thriving and sustainable planet where every individual takes responsibility for mindful resource use, respects nature, and works collaboratively for the betterment of society. A world of peace and harmony, where diversity thrives, planetary crises are resolved, and communities and organizations unite to combat climate change, foster resilience, and live in harmony with the environment for generations to come.

Mission

To empower children and communities with the knowledge, tools, resources, and actionable solutions for climate awareness and sustainability. Through education, research, and collaboration, we bridge the gap between academic insights and real-world action, enabling a collective effort towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), building adaptation and resilience, and combating the climate crisis.


Our Values

Scientific Integrity

We ensure that all information shared is accurate, scientific, and factual, maintaining the highest standards of credibility.

Planetary Responsibility

We take collective ownership for creating a sustainable planet by promoting eco-conscious actions and making a positive impact on the environment.

Resource Efficiency

Our programs are designed to utilize minimal resources while maximizing impact, ensuring sustainability at every step.

Energy and Resource Consciousness

We prioritize mindful use of energy and resources, continuously seeking to reduce our ecological footprint in all our initiatives.

Power of Education

We believe that education has the highest potential to drive meaningful change, empowering individuals and communities to act for a sustainable future.

What is the Climate Clock?
The Climate Clock deadline shows how long we have left until the Earth's carbon budget runs out. It visually represents the time remaining to stay within our carbon budget and avoid surpassing critical temperature thresholds. The clock serves as a stark reminder of the urgency to take immediate and substantial action to reduce CO₂ emissions.
