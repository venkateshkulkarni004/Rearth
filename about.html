REarth

Responsible Earthlings for a Sustainable Planet is dedicated to fostering climate education, awareness, action, and driving positive change. We empower individuals, especially youth, with knowledge and tools to promote sustainability and eco-conscious actions. Through education, collaboration, and innovation, we bridge the gap between academic insights and real-world climate solutions. Our programs, such as the SDG Star School, Women in Climate Leadership and other educational workshops, engage communities to take collective action towards a sustainable future.

Vision

A thriving and sustainable planet where every individual takes responsibility for mindful resource use, respects nature, and works collaboratively for the betterment of society. A world of peace and harmony, where diversity thrives, planetary crises are resolved, and communities and organizations unite to combat climate change, foster resilience, and live in harmony with the environment for generations to come.

Mission

To empower children and communities with the knowledge, tools, resources, and actionable solutions for climate awareness and sustainability. Through education, research, and collaboration, we bridge the gap between academic insights and real-world action, enabling a collective effort towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), building adaptation and resilience, and combating the climate crisis.


Our Values

Integrity & Education

We ensure accurate, scientific information and believe education drives meaningful change for sustainability.

Planetary Responsibility

We promote eco-conscious actions for a sustainable planet.

Resource Efficiency

Our programs maximize impact with minimal resources.

Energy Consciousness

We prioritize mindful energy use to reduce our ecological footprint.
